Unit 5 Case Studies

Real-World Applications

Analyze how inheritance patterns and genetic principles apply to medical cases and biological phenomena.

Case Study 1: Hereditary Disease

Background

A family shows an unusual pattern of early-onset hearing loss.

Clinical Data

  • Multiple generations affected
  • Males and females affected equally
  • Every affected person has affected parent
  • Variable age of onset

Analysis Questions

  1. What is the inheritance pattern?
  2. What is the risk for future children?
  3. How would you confirm the diagnosis?

Case Study 2: Multiple Alleles

Background

Blood type incompatibility in pregnancy.

Clinical Information

  • Mother type O, father type AB
  • Previous pregnancy complications
  • Rh factor consideration
  • Family history

Analysis Questions

  1. What are possible fetal blood types?
  2. What are the medical concerns?
  3. What preventive measures exist?

Case Study 3: Chromosomal Analysis

Background

Prenatal screening results indicate potential chromosomal abnormality.

Test Results

  • Abnormal karyotype
  • Maternal age 38
  • Screening markers
  • Family history

Analysis Questions

  1. What additional tests are needed?
  2. How did this occur?
  3. What are the implications?

Case Study 4: Sex-Linked Traits

Background

Color blindness patterns in extended family.

Family Data

  • Multiple affected males
  • No male-to-male transmission
  • Unaffected carrier females
  • Pedigree analysis

Analysis Questions

  1. Explain inheritance pattern
  2. Calculate carrier probability
  3. Predict future generations

Case Analysis Tips

  • Draw pedigrees
  • List all possibilities
  • Consider exceptions
  • Support conclusions
  • Show calculations
