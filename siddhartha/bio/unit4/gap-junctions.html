Gap Junctions

Essential Understanding

Gap junctions are specialized channel proteins that allow direct communication between adjacent cells through the exchange of small molecules and ions.

Quick Check

Why are gap junctions important in cardiac tissue?

Gap junctions allow electrical coupling between cardiac cells, enabling synchronized contraction of heart muscle through rapid propagation of action potentials.
