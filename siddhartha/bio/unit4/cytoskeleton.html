Cytoskeletal Changes

Essential Understanding

Cell signaling pathways can rapidly reorganize the cytoskeleton, leading to changes in cell shape, movement, and internal organization.

Quick Check

How do Rho GTPases control cell movement?

Rho GTPases regulate actin dynamics and focal adhesion formation, coordinating cytoskeletal reorganization to create directed cell movement through lamellipodia and stress fiber formation.
