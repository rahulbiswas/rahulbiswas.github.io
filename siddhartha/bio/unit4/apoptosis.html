Programmed Cell Death (Apoptosis)

Essential Understanding

Apoptosis is a regulated form of cell death triggered by specific signals, essential for development, tissue homeostasis, and removal of damaged cells.

Quick Check

How do the intrinsic and extrinsic pathways differ in activation?

The extrinsic pathway is activated by external death ligands binding to receptors, while the intrinsic pathway responds to internal stress signals through mitochondrial changes.
