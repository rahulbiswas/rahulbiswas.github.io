Cell Cycle Regulation

Essential Understanding

Cell signaling pathways control cell cycle progression through cyclins, CDKs, and checkpoints, ensuring proper cell division and growth.

Quick Check

Why are cell cycle checkpoints important?

Checkpoints ensure accurate DNA replication and chromosome segregation by monitoring cell conditions and stopping cycle progression if problems are detected, preventing genetic damage.
