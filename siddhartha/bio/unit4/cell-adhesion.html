Cell Adhesion

Essential Understanding

Cell adhesion molecules facilitate cell-cell and cell-matrix interactions, playing crucial roles in tissue organization, signaling, and cellular communication.

Types of Adhesion Molecules

Cadherins

  • Calcium-dependent adhesion
  • Homophilic binding
  • Tissue-specific expression
  • Cytoskeletal connection

Integrins

  • Cell-matrix adhesion
  • Heterodimeric structure
  • Signal transduction
  • Bidirectional signaling

Immunoglobulin Superfamily

  • Diverse recognition
  • Neural connections
  • Immune responses
  • Cell recognition

Quick Check

How do integrins function in both adhesion and signaling?

Integrins bind extracellular matrix proteins for adhesion while also transmitting signals bidirectionally across the membrane, coordinating cell behavior with environmental conditions.
