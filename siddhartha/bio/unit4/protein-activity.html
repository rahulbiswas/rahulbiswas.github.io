Protein Activity Changes

Essential Understanding

Cell signaling pathways regulate protein activity through various post-translational modifications and conformational changes, leading to rapid cellular responses.

Post-translational Modifications

Phosphorylation

  • Changes protein charge
  • Affects protein function

Quick Check

Why is protein phosphorylation such a common regulatory mechanism?

Phosphorylation is reversible, rapid, and can dramatically change protein function by altering charge, conformation, and binding properties, making it ideal for dynamic cellular regulation.
