Cancer & Gene Expression

Essential Understanding

Cancer develops through changes in gene expression that disrupt normal cell growth and division controls, often involving multiple mutations in key regulatory genes.

Quick Check

Why do cancer cells typically need multiple mutations to develop?

Multiple mutations are needed to overcome various cellular safeguards against uncontrolled growth, including cell cycle checkpoints, DNA repair, and programmed cell death mechanisms.
