Common Lab Issues

  • Air bubble formation - use degassed solutions
  • Temperature fluctuations - maintain water bath
  • Poor organism selection - check viability
  • Incorrect volume measurements - calibrate pipettes

Photosynthesis Experiments

Chloroplast Isolation

Key steps for successful isolation:

  • Use isotonic buffer with proper pH
  • Keep tissue cold and work quickly
  • Gentle homogenization to prevent damage
  • Differential centrifugation steps
  • Protect from light when not measuring

Safety Procedures

Essential Lab Safety

  • Wear proper PPE at all times
  • Handle chemicals with care
  • Know waste disposal protocols

Troubleshooting Guide

Common issues and solutions:

  • No activity: Check reagent freshness and pH
  • Variable results: Improve temperature control
  • Poor replication: Standardize technique
  • Contamination: Review sterile technique
