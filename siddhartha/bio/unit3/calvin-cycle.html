Calvin Cycle

Essential Understanding

The Calvin cycle uses ATP and NADPH from light-dependent reactions to fix carbon dioxide into glucose, completing photosynthesis.

Quick Check

Why is RuBisCO considered inefficient?

RuBisCO can bind both CO2 and O2, sometimes initiating photorespiration instead of carbon fixation, wasting energy. It's also a relatively slow enzyme.
