Chemiosmosis & ATP Synthesis

Essential Understanding

Chemiosmosis couples the proton gradient generated by the electron transport chain to ATP synthesis through ATP synthase, completing cellular respiration.

Quick Check

How does ATP synthase couple proton flow to ATP synthesis?

Proton flow through F0 drives rotation of the central stalk, causing conformational changes in F1 that enable ATP synthesis through the binding change mechanism.
