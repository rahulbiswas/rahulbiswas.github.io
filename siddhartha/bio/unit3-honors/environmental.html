Environmental Impacts

Essential Understanding

Environmental changes affect cellular energetics and photosynthesis, which in turn impact ecosystem function and global carbon cycling.

Climate Change Effects

Temperature Impacts

  • Enzyme function changes
  • Photosynthetic efficiency
  • Respiration rates increase
  • Carbon balance shifts

CO2 Effects

  • Enhanced photosynthesis
  • Water use efficiency
  • Nutrient limitations
  • Plant growth changes

Quick Check

How does increased CO2 affect plant growth?

While increased CO2 can enhance photosynthesis (CO2 fertilization effect), benefits may be limited by other factors like water availability, nutrients, and temperature stress.
