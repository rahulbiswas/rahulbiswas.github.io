Fermentation

Essential Understanding

Fermentation is an anaerobic process that allows cells to produce ATP when oxygen is unavailable, though less efficiently than aerobic respiration.

Quick Check

Why do muscles switch to fermentation during intense exercise?

When oxygen demand exceeds supply during intense exercise, muscles switch to lactic acid fermentation to maintain ATP production, though this leads to lactic acid buildup and fatigue.
