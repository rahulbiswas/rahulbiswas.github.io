Cellular Respiration Problems

1. Calculate ATP yield from one glucose molecule:

  • How many from glycolysis?
  • How many from Krebs cycle?
  • How many from ETC?
  • What's the total yield?
  • Glycolysis: 2 ATP net
  • Krebs cycle: 2 ATP
  • ETC: ~34 ATP
  • Total: ~38 ATP per glucose

2. Compare energy yields:

  • Aerobic respiration
  • Lactic acid fermentation
  • Alcoholic fermentation
  • Aerobic: ~38 ATP
  • Lactic acid: 2 ATP
  • Alcoholic: 2 ATP
  • Aerobic is most efficient!
