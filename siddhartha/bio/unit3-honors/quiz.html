Multiple Choice

1. Which process requires oxygen?

  • a) Glycolysis
  • b) Fermentation
  • c) Electron transport chain
  • d) Light-dependent reactions

c) Electron transport chain - Oxygen serves as the final electron acceptor in aerobic respiration.

2. What is produced in the light reactions?

  • a) Glucose and CO2
  • b) ATP and NADPH
  • c) Pyruvate and NADH
  • d) RuBP and G3P

b) ATP and NADPH - These energy carriers power the Calvin cycle.

Short Answer

3. Compare aerobic respiration and fermentation:

  • Aerobic uses oxygen, fermentation doesn't
  • Aerobic produces ~38 ATP, fermentation only 2
  • Both start with glycolysis
  • Different final electron acceptors

4. Explain how chemiosmosis works:

  • H+ pumped across membrane
  • Creates concentration gradient
  • H+ flows through ATP synthase
  • Flow powers ATP production
