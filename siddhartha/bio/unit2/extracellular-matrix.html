Extracellular Matrix

Essential Understanding

The extracellular matrix (ECM) is a complex network of proteins and polysaccharides that provides structural and biochemical support to surrounding cells.

Quick Check

How does the ECM influence cell behavior?

The ECM provides both physical and chemical signals through integrin binding and growth factor presentation, influencing cell survival, migration, differentiation, and function.
