Quick Check

What makes receptor-mediated endocytosis more efficient than pinocytosis?

Receptor-mediated endocytosis uses specific receptors to concentrate desired molecules in coated pits, making it more selective and efficient than the nonspecific fluid uptake of pinocytosis.
