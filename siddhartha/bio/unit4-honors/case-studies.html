Case Study 1: Cancer Development

Background

A young patient develops retinoblastoma (eye tumor) despite no family history.

Clinical Data

  • Tumor only in one eye
  • No inherited mutations found
  • Cell cycle checkpoints disrupted
  • Multiple mutations present

Analysis Questions

  1. Why do mutations accumulate?
  2. How do checkpoint failures lead to cancer?
  3. What treatment approaches might work?

Case Study 2: Sex Determination

Background

Investigation of sea turtle population changes due to climate warming.

Environmental Data

  • Rising nest temperatures
  • Skewed sex ratios
  • Population changes
  • Conservation concerns

Analysis Questions

  1. How does temperature affect sex?
  2. What are the population impacts?
  3. What conservation steps help?

Case Study 3: Genetic Testing

Background

Family seeking genetic counseling for colorblindness pattern.

Family History

  • Multiple affected males
  • Unaffected carrier females
  • Complex inheritance pattern
  • Testing options available

Analysis Questions

  1. What inheritance pattern is shown?
  2. How to calculate carrier probability?
  3. What counseling advice applies?
