Fed Goals
- Promote maximum employment
- Stabilize prices
- Promote economic growth

Monetary Policy
- Recession would naturally lead to price decrease
- Increased money supply instead increases prices
- Overheated economy would lead to further price hikes
- Decreased money supply instead decreases prices
- Liquidity trap = fed funds rate already close to 0
- Phillips curve; x unemployment ; y inflation
- AD + -> Inflation +
- AS + -> Curve moves away from origin
