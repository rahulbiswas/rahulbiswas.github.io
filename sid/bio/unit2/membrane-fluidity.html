Membrane Fluidity

Essential Understanding

Membrane fluidity is crucial for cell function and is regulated by lipid composition, temperature, and cholesterol content.

Quick Check

How does cholesterol help maintain membrane fluidity?

Cholesterol prevents fatty acid crystallization at low temperatures while reducing membrane fluidity at high temperatures, acting as a buffer for membrane fluidity.
