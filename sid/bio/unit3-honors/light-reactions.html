Light-Dependent Reactions

Essential Understanding

The light-dependent reactions of photosynthesis convert light energy into chemical energy (ATP and NADPH) while producing oxygen as a byproduct.

Quick Check

Why is water essential for photosynthesis?

Water provides electrons to replace those excited by light in PSII, generates protons for the H+ gradient, and produces oxygen as a byproduct when split during the light-dependent reactions.
