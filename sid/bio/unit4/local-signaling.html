Local Signaling

Essential Understanding

Local signaling involves communication between nearby cells through direct contact or short-range diffusible molecules, enabling precise coordination of cellular activities.

Types of Local Signaling

Paracrine Signaling

  • Diffusible factors
  • Short-range effects
  • Target nearby cells
  • Rapid response

Autocrine Signaling

  • Self-signaling
  • Cell produces and responds
  • Feedback loops
  • Growth regulation

Juxtacrine Signaling

  • Direct cell contact
  • Membrane-bound ligands
  • Surface receptors
  • Cell-cell recognition

Signaling Molecules

Growth Factors

  • FGF family
  • EGF
  • VEGF

Quick Check

What distinguishes paracrine from autocrine signaling?

In paracrine signaling, cells affect nearby target cells, while in autocrine signaling, cells respond to signals they themselves produce.
