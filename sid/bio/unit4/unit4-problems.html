Signal Transduction Problems

1. A cell surface receptor is activated by its ligand. Calculate the signal amplification if:

  • Each receptor activates 100 G proteins
  • Each G protein activates 10 enzyme molecules
  • Each enzyme generates 1000 second messengers

2. Compare the response times of different signaling mechanisms:

  • Ion channel opening
  • GPCR activation
  • Gene expression changes

  • GPCR cascade: seconds
  • Gene expression: minutes to hours

  • Feedback: phosphatase activation, receptor downregulation
