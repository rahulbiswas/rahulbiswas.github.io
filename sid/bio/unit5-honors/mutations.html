Types of Mutations

Essential Understanding

Mutations are changes in DNA sequence that can affect gene function and expression, ranging from single base changes to large chromosomal alterations.

Quick Check

Why are frameshift mutations often more severe than point mutations?

Frameshift mutations change the reading frame of all downstream codons, usually resulting in a completely different amino acid sequence and non-functional protein, while point mutations affect only one codon.
