Epigenetic Modifications

Essential Understanding

Epigenetics involves changes in gene expression that don't alter DNA sequence but can be inherited, allowing cells to respond to environmental factors and maintain specialized functions.

Quick Check

How can environmental factors affect gene expression without changing DNA sequence?

Environmental factors can trigger epigenetic modifications like DNA methylation and histone modifications that alter gene accessibility and expression without changing the underlying genetic code.
