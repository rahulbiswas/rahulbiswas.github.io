Control of Gene Expression

Essential Understanding

Cells control when and how much protein is made from each gene through multiple levels of regulation, from DNA accessibility to protein modification.

Quick Check

Why do cells need multiple levels of gene regulation?

Multiple control points allow cells to fine-tune gene expression precisely, respond quickly to changes, and maintain tight control over protein production while conserving energy and resources.
