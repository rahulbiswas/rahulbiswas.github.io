Short Answer

3. Explain how phospholipids form cell membranes:

Phospholipids are amphipathic molecules that spontaneously form bilayers with hydrophilic heads facing water and hydrophobic tails facing inward.

4. Compare competitive and noncompetitive inhibition:

Competitive inhibitors bind active site and affect Km only; noncompetitive inhibitors bind elsewhere and decrease Vmax only.
