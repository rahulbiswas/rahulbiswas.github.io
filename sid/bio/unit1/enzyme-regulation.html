Enzyme Regulation

Essential Understanding

Cells regulate enzyme activity through multiple mechanisms to control metabolic pathways and respond to changing conditions.

Allosteric Regulation

Allosteric Activators

  • Increase enzyme activity
  • Bind at regulatory site
  • Stabilize active conformation
  • Example: BPG with hemoglobin

Allosteric Inhibitors

  • Decrease enzyme activity
  • Often end product of pathway
  • Feedback inhibition
  • Example: CTP inhibiting ATCase

Covalent Modification

Types

  • Phosphorylation
  • Glycosylation
  • Ubiquitination
  • Acetylation

Phosphorylation

  • Added by kinases
  • Removed by phosphatases
  • Can activate or inhibit
  • Reversible regulation

Quick Check

Why is feedback inhibition important in metabolic pathways?

Feedback inhibition prevents waste of resources by stopping production when product levels are high, maintaining homeostasis and conserving energy.
