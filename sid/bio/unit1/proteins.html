Proteins

Essential Understanding

Proteins are complex macromolecules made of amino acids that perform most cellular functions, from enzymes to structural support.

Quick Check

Why does heating an egg cause it to become solid?

Heat denatures the proteins in egg white, causing them to unfold and expose hydrophobic regions. These regions interact and form new bonds, creating a solid network structure.
