Probability in Genetics

Essential Understanding

Probability rules help predict inheritance patterns and calculate the likelihood of specific genetic outcomes in breeding and genetic counseling.

Basic Probability Rules

Product Rule

  • Independent events multiply
  • AND situations
  • Multiple traits
  • Sequential events

Sum Rule

  • Alternative outcomes add
  • OR situations
  • Multiple possibilities
  • Mutually exclusive events

Quick Check

If a trait has a 1/4 chance of appearing in one child, what's the probability of it appearing in two children?

Use the product rule: (1/4) √ó (1/4) = 1/16, because each birth is an independent event.
