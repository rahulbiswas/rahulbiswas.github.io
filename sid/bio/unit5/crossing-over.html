Crossing Over

Essential Understanding

Crossing over is the exchange of genetic material between homologous chromosomes during meiosis I, creating new combinations of alleles and increasing genetic diversity.

Quick Check

Why is crossing over important for evolution?

Crossing over creates new combinations of alleles, increasing genetic diversity within populations and providing more variation for natural selection to act upon.
