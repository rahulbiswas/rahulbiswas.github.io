Gene Linkage & Mapping

Essential Understanding

Gene linkage occurs when genes are located close together on the same chromosome, and genetic mapping uses recombination frequencies to determine relative gene positions.

Quick Check

How does recombination frequency relate to map distance?

Map distance in centimorgans equals the recombination frequency percentage (1 cM = 1% recombination), though this relationship is not perfectly linear for larger distances due to multiple crossovers.
