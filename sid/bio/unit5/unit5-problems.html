Mendelian Genetics Problems

1. In pea plants, tall (T) is dominant over short (t). Cross Tt √ó tt:

  • Determine genotype ratio
  • Determine phenotype ratio
  • Calculate probability of short offspring

Solution:

  • Genotype ratio: 1 Tt : 1 tt
  • Phenotype ratio: 1 tall : 1 short
  • Probability of short = 50% or 1/2

Non-Mendelian Inheritance

3. Calculate possible blood type offspring:

  • Phenotypes: Type A, B, AB, O
